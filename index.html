To Coda

The Musical Group

To Coda is a funky group from Atlanta and we were truly formed in a day. Shocking yes but the four of them are inseparable to this day. Mango the summer, Kango the guitarist, Rango the sad pianist and Gango the singer who cannot stop singing. They weren’t sure what style of music they made so they made all kinds Australian Surfer Rock, Justin Bieber covers, Britney covers, YouTubers music covers, and non lyrical music for video games. They truly started to flourish upon a certain day. Halloween when they dressed up as a herd of hotdogs and that was their final form, also because the hotdog suits refused to come off. To Coda is the best band ever.
