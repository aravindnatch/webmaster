PATHWAY

Lambert Engineering

The first year course, designed to introduce students to many different aspects of engineering and build the fundamentals they’ll need. The second year course, Concepts, allows students to continue working on their 3D modeling skills while introducing more of the equipment in the shop. The third year course, Applications, is more open-ended, with students choosing several larger projects to work on that incorporate their skills and interests.

Lambert Engineering is fortunate to have a brand new classroom/lab space (renovated Summer 2016) which serves as the computer lab, basic workspace, home of multiple 3D printers, and storage for engineering activities. There is also a complete shop just down the hall containing a wide variety of equipment specifically for the engineering classes, ranging from a laser cutter and plasma cutter to a CNC mill, CNC lathe, and CNC router. Students have the opportunity, over the course of their time in engineering, to learn to operate different pieces of specialized equipment.
