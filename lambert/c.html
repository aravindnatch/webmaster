Music

 The music that was provided on the website was created and owned by TSA Alumni Joel Joseprabu. He gave us verbal permission to use his music for this project.

His soundcloud page can be found at: https://soundcloud.com/joel-joseprabu.


Images

Throughout this entire project, we have consistently used images/artwork from two different sources.

Source 1:

Our first source was from Joel Joseprabu in which we used his artwork, with his verbal permission, which is also publically posted on his soundcloud page.

His soundcloud page can be found at: https://soundcloud.com/joel-joseprabu.

Source 2:

Most of the stock imagery was found from a website called Pexels. Pexels is a free-to-use stock image website with a large library of images. This site can be found at https://pexels.com.
